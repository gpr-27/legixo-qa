# Fictional statute excerpt — “Riverside Commercial Courts Act (sample text only)”

**Section 14 — Mediation before trial**

A commercial suit above five lakh fictional rupees must go to **mandatory mediation** for 30 days unless both parties waive in writing.

**Section 22 — Interest on delayed payments**

Where a contract fixes no rate, simple interest at **9% per year** may be awarded on admitted dues from the date of demand until payment.
